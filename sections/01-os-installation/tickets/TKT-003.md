# TKT-003: Additional hard drive fitted to a workstation needs initialising and partitioning before use

**Status:** Open  
**Priority:** Low 
**System:** Freshdesk

---

## Resolution Steps
1. Confirmed that disk is identified in Disk Manager 
2. Initialised the disk as MBR as it was a small drive 
3. Set as a New Simple Volume, assigned drive letter D: and performed a quick format
